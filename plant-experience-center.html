An Immersive
Botanical Archive

Far beyond a traditional nursery, the Alipore Experience Center is designed as a living gallery. We invite architects, interior designers, and collectors to walk through staggered glasshouses, bonsai viewing decks, and rare specimen yards to visualize the scale, texture, and character of the plants in their ideal environment.
